GH Insight
Quality of traffic is compounding — revenue is outpacing sessions.

Organic revenue -8.4% to ₹3.68 L on sessions -9.0% and orders -17.4%, with AOV +10.9% (₹2,763). On visibility, GSC clicks -15.9% on +3.5% impressions → site CTR 0.87%; the lever is title/meta on high-impression collection & PDP pages.

Where to act: returning visitors convert at 3.5% vs 1.2% for new — closing the new-visitor gap is the biggest lever. In paid, Google Shopping is at 0.0 ROAS (₹1,398) and should be paused.

Insight
Returning organic users convert 2.9× higher than new — protect the retention loop.

Returning visitors deliver 71% of organic revenue from 44% of sessions. Lower bounce (24% vs 28%), higher depth (9.7 vs 6.4 pages) and ~1.8× session length. With new-visitor CVR at just 1.2%, the priority is converting first-time organic traffic: faster PDPs, clearer sizing, and a first-order incentive.
